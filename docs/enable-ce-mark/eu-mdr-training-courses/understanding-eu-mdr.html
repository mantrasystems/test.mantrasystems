About this course

  1. We have designed this training course for people who are new to the EU MDR and who want to get started in building a regulatory strategy.

  2. Instant, on-demand and unlimited access to videos and downloads along with updates and additions to the existing training material.

  3. This course is presented by the CEO of Mantra Systems, Dr Paul Hercock, who has extensive knowledge of the MDR. He is an experienced presenter of clinical and technical training.

  4. You'll take control of your regulatory strategy and manage individual areas of the EU MDR with confidence.

About our courses

  1. Our training courses are broken up into chapters and short, streamable videos which save your progress automatically. Downloadable documents are included to support your learning.

  2. All our training courses are on-demand and can be completed to your schedule in the comfort of your own workspace. You won't have travel or accommodation costs, or any other expenses usually incurred whilst away from your workspace.

  3. You will have unlimited access to your selected training and if we need to update or add anything to the training course material, we will let you know by email.

  4. Our EnableCE platform offers training, templates and resources that work together to provide the complete support package. Mantra Systems also offers a range of consulting services, free resources such as downloadable White Papers, industry focused articles and an EU MDR Compliance Guide.
